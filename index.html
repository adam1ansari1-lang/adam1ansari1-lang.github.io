Dalitz Plots & Particle Physics
A Python-based analysis of three-body particle decay using Dalitz plots — a technique central to modern high-energy physics experiments. Modelling Air Resistance & Motion
A numerical physics simulation modelling projectile motion under realistic air resistance conditions. Solves the coupled differential equations of motion using Python, comparing drag models (linear and quadratic) across varying initial conditions such as launch angle, velocity, and air density. Visualises trajectory differences between idealised and real-world motion — a core topic in classical mechanics covered in my degree at Warwick.
